Overview

The purpose of this website is to be an online cookbook for recipes that I learn and like! These recipes range from desserts to dinner foods to party foods.

My purpose is to have a lasting repository with recipes that are fun and simple to make, and taste good! I also appreciate the flexibility a digital cookbook offers in tweaking recipes regularly..

Audience

My cookbook is for people who want a simple recipe that tastes good for common and enjoyable foods. This includes college students on a budget, families with lots of mouths to feed, or anyone else!

Dynamic elements

A dynamic element I want to incorporate is being able to filter or search by food type, such as breakfast, lunch, dessert, and so forth. I also want to add a servings adjuster so people cook according to however many servings they want.
